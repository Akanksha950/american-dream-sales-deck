Design Rationale

The "I Need to Be Here" Moment

The sharpest interaction in this deck is the Live Retail Floor Map in the Leasing chapter — and here is exactly why it earns that reaction.

📍
The Prospect Sees Their Exact Space
Rather than describing zones abstractly, the prospect clicks a specific zone on the floor plan. They see their footprint, their neighbors, their foot-traffic position — all at once. It's the difference between being told about an opportunity and physically standing in it.
⚡
No Salesperson Required
The zone detail panel opens instantly with leasing specs, dwell-time data, and a direct "Inquire" CTA. The deck closes its own deal. A prospect exploring alone at midnight can go from curiosity to inquiry in under 60 seconds.
🎯
Every Zone Tells a Different Story
A luxury tenant sees HHI data and tax advantage. A pop-up brand sees flexible terms and peak traffic. A sponsor sees naming rights visibility. One map — nine distinct pitches — all instantly accessible.
How This Was Built

Tools & Approach

🖥
Pure HTML / CSS / JavaScript
Single self-contained file. Zero dependencies, zero build step. Deploy by dropping index.html into any static host — GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify. Loads in under 2 seconds.
🤖
AI-Assisted Design & Content
Claude AI used throughout: copywriting all commercial pitch content, generating the SVG floor map layout, writing the zone-level leasing narratives, and structuring the emotional arc across chapters.
🎨
Digideck Format — Faithfully
Splash gate → Chapter tabs → Filmstrip → Full-screen slides → Non-linear hub → Sub-slides within chapters. The viewer drives the journey, not the deck.
📈
What I Would Add With More Time
Real autoplay video backgrounds. Animated data visualisations (live footfall charts). A PDF export button. A "Share this chapter" link that deep-links into a specific chapter for tailored prospect follow-up.
